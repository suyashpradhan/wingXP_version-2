DIGITAL DESIGN

Visual communication is one of the most powerful ways of expressing an idea in a universal language. In this digital age, there are tools that help you visualise and effectively communicate your thoughts using still and moving graphics. The objective of Digital Design club help in giving exposure of different design tools and other design skills.

TECH TALK

The club aims to promote innovation among its members as they seek out technological know-how and solutions to daily life challenges that may arise at home or in a school environment. It helps students improve their design capabilities and their skills with communication and information technology.

SMARTER MINDS

A faster, accurate and complexity analyzing mind being renders understanding of the world & concepts around which makes you smarter. This club enables student smart by practicing & solving mind wriggling problems, puzzles and questions, which help in sharpening your ability to analyse and evaluate better. A smarter you is going to be your best companion today and tomorrow.

ENVIRONMENT

The club gives students the platform they need to make their contributions towards preserving the environment. To create awareness of environmental issues, such as protection, conservation, preservation, and restoration, with an emphasis on educating and empowering students they learn techniques, floral designs and are taught the concept of reduce, reuse and recycle.

CODING

Coding Club aims to nurture creative and programming skills in students, helping them sharpen their research and presentation skills. Students are also introduced to website & app building and design among other activities. The benefits of learning coding at a young age are enormous and include the development of problem-solving skills, computational thinking, persistence, collaboration, and a deeper understanding of a range of mathematical concepts and language.

ASTRONOMY

The Astronomy Club is formed to inculcate scientific temper among the students by introducing concepts of Science through a process of inquiry, experimentation and analysis. The activities are spread over a period of one year and these may include sky observation, interactive multimedia session, and visits to the Planetarium, etc.

ENGLISH EXPRESSION

The most classical form of expression is still perhaps the most powerful. One slogan, one letter, one blog, one book has a potential to create big impact. The main objective of this iclub is to help you in developing your language skills and empower you with a flair for expressing your thoughts incisively in words.

UNDERSTANDING NEWS

The Mission is to create a more sophisticated and more talented group of students with a vast and deep knowledge in different areas. In order to achieve this, most important thing is to be informed of updated News and also understanding its impact on social structures.
