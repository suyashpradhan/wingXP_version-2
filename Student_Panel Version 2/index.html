Why?

To Give World Class Co-Curriculars

In modern times schools need to emphasise on Co-Curriculars to make students effective and impactful. A restructured framework is to have bigger role of Co-Curriculars without disrupting the core curriculum boundaries.

What?

A Network of Experts

WingXP is a network of experts that help schools to offer Co-Curriculars of choice, interest and flair to its students.

How?

Taking School Clubs Online

WingXP takes the school club structure online based on the 3 XP framework -
1.Experts Guided Exploration
2. Experiencing with Experts
3. Real world Expressions.
This helps the schools to give the best co curriculars to the students
